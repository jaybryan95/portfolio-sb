BuySight — Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 3, 2026 · Last updated: May 3, 2026

BuySight (the “App”) is operated by Gimmi Group (“we”, “us”, or “our”). BuySight is a receipt and expense tracking app: you scan receipts, and we help extract purchase details, categorize spending, and organize records. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use BuySight. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

Camera and receipt images

The App declares android.permission.CAMERA so you can capture receipt photos with your device camera for scanning and expense tracking. We do not access your camera in the background; it is used when you choose a camera-based action in the App.

You may decline camera permission or revoke it in Settings → Apps → BuySight → Permissions. Without camera access, features that require taking or retaking receipt photos may be unavailable; you may still be able to use other entry paths the App offers (such as choosing images from your gallery), if applicable.

Receipt images you capture or upload are used to extract and organize purchase information as described below. They may be processed on your device and/or on our systems or those of service providers we use to run the App.

1. Information we collect

BuySight collects information to provide and improve the service.

Information you provide

When using BuySight, you may provide:

  • Receipt or invoice images you upload or capture with the camera;
  • Purchase information extracted from those receipts;
  • Account information such as your email address, if account creation or sign-in is required.

Automatically collected information

We may automatically collect limited technical information such as:

  • Device type;
  • App usage data;
  • Error logs and performance data.

This information helps us improve the reliability and performance of the App.

2. How we use your information

We use collected information to:

  • Extract and organize purchase data from receipts;
  • Categorize and analyze spending;
  • Improve the accuracy and functionality of BuySight;
  • Provide customer support;
  • Maintain and improve app performance.

3. Receipt processing

When you upload or capture a receipt, BuySight may use automated technologies, including AI-powered data extraction, to read and organize receipt information. Images may be temporarily processed to extract purchase details. In many cases, only extracted structured data is stored rather than the original image, depending on how the feature is implemented and what you choose to keep.

BuySight does not use your uploaded receipts to train public AI models without your consent.

4. Data storage

Your extracted receipt data may be stored securely so you can view purchase history, analyze spending patterns, and access insights and reports. We implement reasonable security measures to help protect user data.

5. Data sharing

BuySight does not sell or rent your personal data. We may share limited information with trusted service providers that help operate the App (such as cloud hosting or analytics). Those providers are required to protect your data and use it only to provide services to us.

6. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no digital system can guarantee complete security.

7. Your rights

You may request to access your stored data, delete your account or stored data, or contact us regarding privacy concerns. Send requests to the email address below. We will respond in line with applicable law.

8. Children’s privacy

BuySight is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective or “last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after updates may constitute acceptance of the revised policy where permitted by law.
